The Seven Gods of the Realm

Kothar: God of Destruction

A force of inevitable and transformative power, Kothar embodies both ruin and renewal. He represents destruction as a necessary force for change. His influence is felt in the violent devastation of war and the quiet, inexorable decay of nature and life. His followers view destruction not as an end, but as part of a greater cycle—whether through the crumbling of empires, the death of living things, or the disintegration of the physical world. Kothar’s power is one of both chaos and order, teaching that only through breakdown can new growth emerge.

Church

The Church of the Shattered Blade embraces destruction as a catalyst for transformation. Its followers believe that in order to progress, the old must be broken down, creating space for something new. Through rituals focused on controlled ruin, they seek to harness the power of destruction, viewing it not as an end, but as a necessary step in the cycle of creation and renewal.
