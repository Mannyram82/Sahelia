The Sahelian Pantheon

The Seven Gods of the Realm

Kothar: God of Destruction

A force of inevitable and transformative power, Kothar embodies both ruin and renewal. He represents destruction as a necessary force for change. His influence is felt in the violent devastation of war and the quiet, inexorable decay of nature and life. His followers view destruction not as an end, but as part of a greater cycle—whether through the crumbling of empires, the death of living things, or the disintegration of the physical world. Kothar’s power is one of both chaos and order, teaching that only through breakdown can new growth emerge.

Divine Waypoint

The Crumbled Spire: A towering column of shattered stone, cracked with glowing red energy, stands as a monument to Kothar’s power. To seek his trials, one must touch the spire with intent. If accepted, you are transported to a realm of ruin, where only those who endure destruction can earn an audience. Failure brings consequences, but success grants a meeting with Kothar, whose judgment is unwavering, testing both strength and resolve.

Eramé: Goddess of Creation

Embodied in both the untamed forces of nature and the careful craftsmanship of creation, Eramé shapes life, growth, and the world around it. She is the guiding force behind the nurturing of new beginnings and the transformation of the old. Her followers view creation as an endless cycle, where every act of bringing forth life or form is a step toward greater purpose. Through her, all things take shape and evolve, constantly finding meaning in the act of creation.

Morvath: God of Dreams

In both the calm of dreams and the chaos of nightmares, Morvath shapes the landscape of the unconscious mind. His influence extends through the peaceful visions that offer guidance and the dark fears that prompt growth and self-discovery. Followers recognize that both dream and nightmare serve a purpose—dreams enlighten and inspire, while nightmares challenge and reveal hidden truths. Through Morvath, the mind navigates the boundaries of possibility, finding meaning in both the comforting and the unsettling.

Kalara: Goddess of Time

Both the unyielding flow of time and the certainty of destiny are woven together under her influence. Kalara guides the passage of moments, where each second shapes the future, while also governing the fixed events that must come to pass. Followers believe that time moves ever forward, but within it, destiny unfolds, shaping lives and events with inevitable certainty. Through Kalara, every moment is both a step in time’s passage and a piece of a greater, unalterable plan. While time seems fluid and choices may appear free, all roads ultimately lead to the destined end.

Balorin: God of Balance

Justice and harmony are the twin forces Balorin governs, each crucial to maintaining balance in the world. He ensures fairness prevails, upholding what is right and just, while also fostering peace through unity and understanding. His followers believe that justice is not only the correction of wrongs, but the restoration of equilibrium, and that harmony arises when all forces are in balance. Through Balorin, every act of judgment, reconciliation, or compassion contributes to a greater, lasting balance that sustains and unites all of existence, ensuring that neither chaos nor oppression can overwhelm the world.

Illyandra: Goddess of Shadow

The power of darkness and secrets intertwines under Illyandra’s guidance, governing all that is hidden. She embodies the strength found in shadows, where safety lies in what is concealed. Her followers believe not all things should be revealed, as secrecy offers both power and refuge. The timing of revelation is as crucial as the secret itself—hidden truths, shared at the right moment, can reshape events. Through Illyandra, the unseen is honored, with balance between concealment and revelation guiding the world. The hidden holds as much value as the visible, and shadows often conceal the greatest truths.

Garrick: God of Journeys

The journeys of both explorers and those on paths of personal growth are shaped by Garrick’s guidance. He helps wayfarers navigate the vast world, while also inspiring those who seek transformation within themselves. His followers believe that every journey, whether outward or inward, is filled with meaning and purpose. Each step taken, whether across unknown lands or through personal challenges, brings the traveler closer to enlightenment and understanding. Through Garrick, all journeys—whether of discovery or self-realization—lead to growth, wisdom, and a deeper connection to one's true purpose.
